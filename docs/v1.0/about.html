ABOUT US

Dn'D is a digital agency specialized in eCommerce and the integration of third-party solutions (PIM, CRM, ETL). Dn'D advises and supports B2B and B2C merchants in their digital strategy, eCommerce, UX/UI design, CRO, web development and maintenance.

Thanks to its experience since 2004 and its knowledge of the market, the agency develops connectors and extensions for editors wishing to connect their solution to the main CMS on the market.

Thanks to its expertise in Magento and Akeneo, Dn'D was able to develop the connector between the two solutions, officially recognized by publishers and the community: PIMGento, which became Akeneo Connector for Magento 2 in 2019. It enables merchants to automate the import of product catalog data from Akeneo to Magento.

Our team is a certified partner of:

  • Magento partner since 2008
  • Akeneo partner since 2015
  • Adobe since 2018
